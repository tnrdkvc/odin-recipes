Tenstan krompir



Description

Tenstan krompir are traditional Slovenian fried potatoes, usually made with onions.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup lard or shortening
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp chives, if desired

Steps

  1. Wash and boil unpeel potatoes until almost tender.
  2. Cool, peel and slice.
  3. In skillet, saut√© sliced onion in lard or shortening until soft.
  4. Add potatoes, salt, pepper and chives and fry over low heat for about 15 minutes until potatoes are lightly browned, turning occasionally.
